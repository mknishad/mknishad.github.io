About me

I'm a flexible, results-driven Lead Mobile Engineer with over 8 years of experience designing, developing and optimizing high-performance mobile applications. My passion is delivering seamless user experiences while building scalable, secure software.

I've led the development of a GDPR-compliant telehealth app (eDoktor) with secure video consultations, contributed to a major eCommerce platform (Dhamaka Shopping) with real-time data and payment-gateway integrations, and built SDKs for financial services (shurjoPay) and a Learning Management System (Retina LMS).

My architectural approach leans on MVVM, Clean Architecture & Modular Design, always following SOLID principles. Beyond shipping products, I'm an active competitive programmer, sharpening my problem-solving and data-structures muscle on and offline.
